Types of Guided Meditations

Mindfulness Meditation

  1. Focuses on staying present and observing thoughts and feelings without judgment.
    Example: "Notice your breath as it flows in and out..." Body Scan
  2. Guides you to focus on each part of the body, promoting physical relaxation.
    Example: "Bring your attention to your feet. Feel any sensations there..." Loving-Kindness (Metta)
  3. Cultivates compassion for yourself and others by repeating phrases of goodwill.
    Example: "May I be happy. May I be healthy..." Visualization
  4. Uses mental imagery to create a relaxing or inspiring experience.
    Example: "Imagine yourself walking along a calm beach, feeling the warm sand..." Stress Relief
  5. Focuses on calming techniques to reduce anxiety or tension.
    Example: "Breathe deeply and imagine the stress melting away with each exhale." Sleep Meditation
  6. Designed to help you drift into sleep with gentle guidance and soothing tones.
    Example: "Allow your body to sink deeper into the mattress..." Focus and Productivity
  7. Helps center your thoughts and enhance concentration for tasks ahead.
    Example: "Visualize yourself working calmly and efficiently..."
